About Us

Founded in 2005 by Vic and Irene Alley, Alleysway began as a dream and became a fitness family.

Located in Bedfordview’s Italian Club, the gym reflects their unique philosophy — doing things the Alley way.
Over the years, Alleysway expanded into a larger space, adding top-tier equipment, a supplement store, massage therapy, and a mesotherapy clinic.

It’s more than a gym — it’s a home where clients are treated like family and lifestyles are transformed.

Our on-site amenities

Coffee & More

Located right inside the gym, Coffee & More offers fresh brews, protein smoothies, and wholesome snacks. It’s the perfect spot to kickstart your workout or relax and recharge after a great session.

Jade's Wellness & Secret Glow

Jade’s Wellness & Secret Glow offers premium hair, beauty, and self-care services right inside the gym - perfect for a post-workout refresh or a full pamper session in a relaxing, stylish, and professional space.

Biokinetics & Chiropractic Care

Recover, realign, and regain strength with expert biokinetics and chiropractic care. Our wellness team helps you manage chronic conditions, improve mobility, and accelerate recovery through personalized, hands-on rehabilitation and movement therapy.

Sports Massage Therapy

Relieve tension, speed up recovery, and treat muscle soreness with targeted sports massage. Our certified therapist offers personalized care to help you move better, feel stronger, and stay pain-free—whether you’re training hard or just need to unwind.

Hyperbaric Chamber

Experience the benefits of advanced recovery technology. After your session, visit us to take advantage of our cutting-edge hyperbaric chamber, designed to deliver increased oxygen to your muscle tissues—promoting faster healing, reduced fatigue, and optimal performance recovery..
